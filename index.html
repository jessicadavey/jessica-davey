Biography

Originally from Batavia, Illinois, Jessica moved to England in 2006 to study jazz and music composition at the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts (founded by former Beatle Paul McCartney). She graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Music, "1st class honours" in 2009.

Since graduation, she has performed internationally as a solo piano vocalist. Her residencies have included The Ritz-Carlton in Osaka, Japan and P&O Cruises, Australia. She also spent several years as a regular at piano bars in Glasgow, Scotland.

Jessica has also written and produced three solo albums. Her songwriting and musical production influences include The Beatles, 1970s singer-songwriters such as Joni Mitchell and Carole King, and Brazilian bossa-nova.

She currently resides in the countryside near Nashville, Tennessee.
